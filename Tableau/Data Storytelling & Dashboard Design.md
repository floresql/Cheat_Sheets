# 3. Data Storytelling & Dashboard Design Cheat Sheet

### 1. Visual Selection Matrix
Choose the right chart based on the data relationship.
*   **Comparison**: Bar Charts (Categorical), Line Charts (Time-series).
*   **Composition**: Treemaps (Hierarchies), Stacked Area (Trends over time).
*   **Distribution**: Histograms (Volume), Box Plots (Outliers/Spread).
*   **Relationship**: Scatter Plots (Correlation), Parallel Coordinates (Multi-variate patterns).

### 2. Dashboard Best Practices Checklist
Ensure your dashboard is useful, not just pretty.
*   **Visual Hierarchy**: Put the most important KPI in the top-left corner.
*   **Data-Ink Ratio**: Remove unnecessary borders, gridlines, and bolding to highlight the data.
*   **Color Theory**: Use color to highlight (e.g., Red for "Action Required"), not just for decoration.
*   **The 5-Second Rule**: A user should understand the main "message" within 5 seconds.

### 3. Stakeholder Tailoring
Match the level of detail to the audience.
*   **Executive**: High-level KPIs, Trends, and "Bottom Line" impact.
*   **Manager**: Departmental performance, Cohort analysis, and Variance to goal.
*   **Analyst/Operator**: Granular row-level data, Filterable dimensions, and Root-cause tools.
